BadBlink Games ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data Loopyballs (package: com.badblinkgames.loopyballs) collects, how we use and share it, and your choices. By installing or using Loopyballs, you agree to this policy.

This applies only to data collected by Loopyballs on Android devices. External links or third-party services have separate privacy policies.

3. Target Audience & Children’s Privacy

Age Restriction: Intended for users aged 13 and older.

Children Under 13: We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Contact us to request deletion if inadvertent collection occurs.

Ad Content: No behaviorally targeted ads for children; only contextual ads shown if under-13 usage detected.

Data Type Purpose & Details

In-App Purchases Processed via Google Play Billing; we store transaction IDs only.
